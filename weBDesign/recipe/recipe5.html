step 4



As I have used cooked sausages once the batter is cooked,

you can remove from the pan. I place on some kitchen towel

to remove some of the oil and once the sticks are cooled you can eat.

If you want a nice dipping sauce, mix together 3 tablespoons of tomato ketchup,

1 tablespoon of gochujang and sugar.

If you want to make more of a meal of it you can cook

some chicken breast in the same way and serve with some fries.
